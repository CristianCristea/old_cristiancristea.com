Project Overview

Mozubi is a learning platform made specifically for german apprentices that will replace the outdated exam preparation material. The platform has 4 entry points ( major parts ) for: students, authors, admins and companies.

The learning platform has a sequential syllabus with topics, articles and video lectures. The students can test their knowledge after every article with quizzes (multiple choice, drag and drop) or take real exam simulations.

I am the main developer and project manager of Mozubi.
