Project Overview

Develop a learning platform for german apprentices that will replace the outdated exam preparation material.

The learning platform has a sequential syllabus with topics and articles to be read and a quiz(multiple choice) for every article.

The project had a team of 4 developers, I developed the landing page, the backend (RoR models, PostgreSQL) and the quiz.
